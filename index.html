Our Practice Areas

Professional Services We Offer

Our firm provides a comprehensive suite of financial and compliance services to individuals, partnerships, private limited companies, and other entities — covering Indian statutory requirements as well as US regulatory frameworks.

All engagements are carried out in accordance with applicable Standards and the Code of Ethics prescribed by the ICAI.

01
📚
Accounting & Bookkeeping
Maintenance of books of accounts, preparation of financial statements, and periodic management reports in accordance with applicable accounting standards.
02
🔍
Audit Services
Statutory audit, internal audit, and tax audit services for companies, LLPs, and other entities as mandated under applicable laws and regulations.
03
📋
Income Tax & GST Filings
Preparation and filing of income tax returns for individuals, firms, and companies; GST registration, return filing, and compliance management.
04
🏛️
Company Incorporation & Compliance
Assistance in incorporation of companies and LLPs, secretarial compliance, annual filings with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, and ongoing regulatory support.
05
🇺🇸
US Bookkeeping & Taxation
Bookkeeping, reconciliation, and tax compliance support for US-based entities and individuals, including preparation of tax workpapers as per US GAAP requirements.
