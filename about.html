About the Firm

Trusted Advice. Reliable Compliance. Clear Outcomes.

Jayalaxmi Kamath & Co. is a Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered in Udupi, Karnataka. We support individuals and businesses with audit, taxation, accounting and compliance services — aligned with the professional standards and ethics prescribed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.

Our firm brings together experienced partners with diverse expertise, enabling us to serve clients across both domestic and international compliance requirements, including US bookkeeping and taxation support.
